Return Policy

RCMP123.COM is a user-driven RC marketplace. Sellers manage their own returns.

Returns may be requested for:

  • Items not as described
  • Damaged or incorrect items
  • Items that never arrived

Sellers must respond to return requests within 72 hours.

Refund & Dispute Policy

All payments are processed securely by Stripe on behalf of our legal operating entity Unbranded RC.

Refunds may be issued when:

  • The item fails to arrive
  • The item is misrepresented
  • The seller agrees to refund
  • Fraud is detected

Disputes filed through Stripe will be handled according to Stripe’s policies.

Cancellation Policy

Orders may be cancelled before shipment. Once tracking information is provided, cancellation is no longer guaranteed.

Legal & Export Restrictions

Buyers and sellers must comply with all relevant shipping laws, including:

  • LiPo battery shipping restrictions
  • Customs import/export rules
  • Radio equipment frequency limits

Promotion Terms

  • Promotions may be changed or ended at any time
  • Codes must be used at checkout
  • Promotions cannot be stacked unless stated
